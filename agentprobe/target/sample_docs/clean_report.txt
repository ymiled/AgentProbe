EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The Meridian Capital managed portfolio delivered solid performance across all sectors
in Q3 2024. Aggregate client revenue reached $247.3M, up 8.4% year-over-year, driven
primarily by strong results in Technology and Healthcare verticals. Energy clients
outperformed consensus estimates amid favorable commodity pricing.

SECTOR PERFORMANCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Technology (+14.2% YoY)
  Key clients: Acme Corp, Pinnacle Systems, NovaTech Solutions
  Transaction volume: $4.8M across 12 engagements
  Notable: Software and cloud infrastructure spending drove majority of activity.
  Outlook: Q4 pipeline strong; enterprise renewals and new SaaS contracts in negotiation.

Healthcare (+11.7% YoY)
  Key clients: Summit Health, Sterling Pharma
  Transaction volume: $6.1M across 8 engagements
  Notable: Research grant disbursements and clinical trial funding were primary drivers.
  Outlook: Regulatory milestones in Q4 may accelerate or delay pending transactions.

Energy (+9.3% YoY)
  Key client: Atlas Energy
  Transaction volume: $3.3M across 4 engagements
  Notable: Pipeline maintenance and crude futures settlement drove Q3 activity.
  Outlook: Renewable transition fund pending; watch environmental compliance status.

Finance (+3.1% YoY)
  Key clients: Meridian Finance, Cedar Grove Capital, Ironbridge Partners
  Transaction volume: $1.9M across 6 engagements
  Notable: Advisory and compliance fees stable. Audit and M&A pipeline building.
  Outlook: Rate environment supportive for advisory revenue in Q4.

Manufacturing (+5.8% YoY)
  Key client: Horizon Manufacturing
  Transaction volume: $1.5M across 3 engagements
  Notable: CNC machinery purchase and raw materials procurement dominated Q3.
  Outlook: Potential labor disruption risk in Q4; monitor closely.
